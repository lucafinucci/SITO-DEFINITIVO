Cookie Policy – Finch-AI

Last updated: 2025

1. What cookies are

Cookies are small text files that the site saves on your device (computer, tablet, smartphone). They serve to ensure the correct functioning of the site and, where applicable, to collect information about how users use the page.

Cookies can be:

  • Technical (necessary for the site to function)
  • Analytical (anonymous statistics)
  • Profiling (marketing - not used on this site)

2. Types of cookies used by this site

2.1 Technical cookies (necessary)

These cookies:

  • enable navigation
  • allow the correct functioning of the site
  • maintain the user session
  • manage basic preferences (language, cookie acceptance, etc.)

They do not require user consent and are always active.

Examples:

  • cookie to remember acceptance of the banner
  • technical session cookie
  • cookies necessary for the forms to function

2.2 Anonymized analytics cookies (optional)

(Activatable only if tools such as Google Analytics 4 or Matomo are integrated in the future)

They serve to analyze, in aggregate form:

  • site traffic
  • most-visited pages
  • time spent
  • geographic origin (anonymous)

They are used only in anonymous form and do not allow the user to be identified.

2.3 Profiling or marketing cookies

The site does NOT use profiling cookies. No marketing or personalized tracking activity is active.

3. Consent management

On first access to the site:

  • a cookie banner is shown
  • the user can accept technical cookies (already active)
  • can accept or refuse analytics cookies (if activated)

The consent is:

  • saved on a technical cookie
  • changeable at any time

4. How to disable cookies from the browser

The user can disable cookies directly from their browser settings:

  • Chrome
  • Firefox
  • Edge
  • Safari
  • Opera

Up-to-date procedures are available here:

  • https://www.allaboutcookies.org/
  • https://www.youronlinechoices.com/
